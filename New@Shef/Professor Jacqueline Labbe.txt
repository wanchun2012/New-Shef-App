Professor Labbe brings to the role of Pro-Vice-Chancellor a combination of outstanding scholarship and extensive experience of University management. Professor Labbe was formerly the Director of the Humanities Research Institute at Warwick and is a leading authority on British writers of the Romantic period. She is a past President of the British Association for Romantic Studies and has acted as both a Strategic and Peer Reviewer for the Arts and Humanities Research Council.
\n
In addition to her role as Head of the Graduate School at Warwick, she serves on the University Council and Senate and the Academic Resources and Finance Committees. She also has extensive international experience, serving on the University International Committee and the Development and Joint Programmes Group which supports the Alliance between Warwick and Monash University in Australia.
